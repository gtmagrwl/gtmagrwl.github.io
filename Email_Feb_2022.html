Lesson Details

I am comfortable teaching GCSE up to A-level or equivalent (AP/SAT/SAT Subject Test etc). Lessons currently take place via Zoom and I am able to offer high quality lessons by using an iPad and input pen to effectively draw diagrams and work through problems with a student. Where possible, students show me their work the same way, but alternative arrangements are possible if this is not an option.


There are two main styles of teaching. Either the student can provide worksheets or material they would like to go through in advance (usually school work or past papers), or I create a plan and bring external resources. Usually, a combination of the two styles works well and this is determined during an initial discovery lesson.
